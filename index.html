Welcome

Welcome to Te Kura Rau Mahara, the Waikato Wearables Research Lab.

We are a multidisciplinary team dedicated to advancing wearable technologies that enhance human capabilities. Our research spans cognitive function, prosthetics, Electroencephalography (EEG), Transcranial Ultrasound Stimulation (TUS), electromyography (EMG), human-robot interaction (HRI), and related fields, integrating scientific innovation with indigenous knowledge to create meaningful and culturally grounded solutions.
